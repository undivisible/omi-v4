What it does

  1. 01

    Memory with evidence

    Every fact keeps a citation back to the moment it came from. Correct it or delete it, and what was derived from it goes too.

  2. 02

    Live meetings

    Transcription and insight while the meeting is still happening.

  3. 03

    Currents

    What matters next, ranked and cited — re-ranked by what you dismiss or accept.

  4. 04

    Voice on a shake

    Shake the cursor, or tap both Shift keys. No window, no hotkey to remember.

  5. 05

    Computer use, approved

    It proposes, you approve, and the outcome lands in an append-only ledger.

  6. 06

    The pendant

    Captures the day over Bluetooth LE. Your phone relays; your desktop remembers.

Open surface

POST/mcp

A public HTTP API and an MCP server, so the tools you already use can ask your second brain too.

  • The same boundary as the app. Every request carries your ID token; every row is scoped to your account before it is read.
  • OpenAI-compatible chat. /v1/chat/completions streams in the shape your clients already speak.
  • See how it is built

Privacy

Your memory lives on your machine.

  • Local source of truth. The cloud keeps only a rebuildable projection for recall.
  • On-device summaries. The workspace scan reads metadata, and on Apple silicon it summarises with no network call.
  • Open source. The line between local and cloud is something you can read.

Features

One assistant, every surface.

A warm, minimal desktop hub opens on “What matters next.” Everything else flows into the same continuous conversation.

Live duplex voice

Talk with Omi in real time over Gemini Live. It hears you, answers aloud, and stays in the flow of the conversation — no push-to-talk turn-taking.

Meeting mode

Live transcription with insights as the meeting happens, then a summary you can act on — captured into the same memory as everything else.

Memory with citations

Every remembered fact is backed by evidence. Ask why Omi believes something and it cites the moment it came from — no vague vibes, no hallucinated history.

Computer use, approved only

On desktop, Omi can act on your machine — but only through actions you explicitly approve. Every step is visible, every action is yours to allow.

Pendant + companion app

The Omi pendant captures your day; the mobile app pairs it, relays audio, and keeps it healthy. Your phone is the bridge, your desktop is the brain.

Channels

Message Omi from Telegram or iMessage (via Blooio). Same assistant, same memory, same conversation — wherever you already talk.

Privacy

Your world is scanned privately.

Onboarding looks at your workspace to understand your world — and summarizes it on your device before anything leaves it. Omi is open source, so the boundary between local and cloud is something you can read, not something you have to trust.

  • On-device summaries. The private scan runs locally and produces summaries on your machine.
  • Evidence you can audit. Memory keeps citations to its sources, so you always know where a fact came from.
  • Approval-gated actions. Computer use never acts without your explicit go-ahead.
